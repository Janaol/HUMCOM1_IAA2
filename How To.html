How to Make Mango Graham Cake

Mango graham cake is a popular dessert in the Philippines. Also called mango float, it's cold, sweet and so easy to make.

Learn how to make this simple yet mouthwatering dessert in 6 simple steps!

Ingredients:
  • 1 unflavored Gelatin
  • 1/4 cups cold Water
  • 6-9 pcs Graham Crackers
  • 1-2 pcs ripe Mango, sliced
  • 1 can Alaska Crema-Asada 370ml, chilled overnight
Instructions:
  1. Pour contents of the gelatin in a heatproof bowl with 59ml cold water. Let it bloom. After so, heat a pot with simmering water. Sit the heatproof bowl over the pot. Melt the gelatin until it becomes liquid. Set aside.
  2. In desired container, line the bottom with 2 pieces of the graham crackers and set aside.
  3. In a clean medium sized mixing bowl, pour chilled can of Alaska Crema-Asada. Whip for about 3 minutes using an electric mixer.
  4. Slowly pour in prepared gelatin into bowl. Stir well.
  5. Pour 1/3 of the mixture over the graham lined container. Place a layer of mango slices. Top with another layer of graham crackers and repeat to create layers ending with a layer of mango slices.
  6. Place in chiller and allow to set. Serve cold.
