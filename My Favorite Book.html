Turtles All the Way Down

By John Green


A story about mental illness with the backdrop of a mystery. John Green's book Turtles All The Way Down uses high school romance, friendship, loss and mystery as the backdrop to a story that takes a look into the brain of a 16-year-old girl suffering from obsessive compulsive disorder.
